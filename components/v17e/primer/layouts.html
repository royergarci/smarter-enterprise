What is v17e?

In a nutshell...

v17e is the mobile optimized version of the v17 design system for ibm.com. It's a framework that will enable you to create a cohesive and engaging digital experience on the ibm.com domain. It's a foundation you can built on top of with extreme flexibility and efficiency. It's a set of tools and resources to to help you focus on what really matters, the customer.
