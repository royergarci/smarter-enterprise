What is v17e?

In a nutshell...

v17e is the mobile optimized version of the v17 design system for ibm.com. It's a framework that will enable you to create a cohesive and engaging digital experience on the ibm.com domain. It's a foundation you can build on top of with extreme flexibility and efficiency. It's a set of tools and resources to help you focus on what really matters, the customer.



Experience

Experience first. While optimizing the experience for mobile, we also made sure that retina screens were considered as well. From layouts to icons, everything has been enhanced to improve the experience without sacrificing performance or load times.

Efficiency

v17e sheds much of the heavy codebase that v17 was burdened with to support older generations of the design. Legacy free means faster performance on mobile devices. A dramatically simplified codebase means sites can be created or converted more quickly.

Everyone

For the first time, we've opened up our codebase to create an open source like ecosystem that allows anyone to subscribe, leverage and modify the framework. Everyone can now benefit. From agencies to individuals, no contribution is too small.
